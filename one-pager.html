🔴 The Problem
  • Creators with 1K–50K followers have no way to monetize locally — brands ignore "small" creators.
  • Local businesses (cafes, gyms, salons) can't afford big influencer agencies. No platform serves them.
  • Deals are negotiated on DMs, no escrow, no accountability — creators get ghosted, brands get poor content.
  • Existing platforms (Winkl, Kofluence) only serve metro cities + macro influencers. Tier-2 India is invisible.
✅ Fluence Fixes This
  • Any creator with 1K+ followers can browse & apply to local deals. Zero minimum.
  • Brands post deals in minutes — specify budget, creators needed, deliverable, timeline.
  • Escrow-first: brand funds deal upfront. Creator posts. Fluence releases payment in 48 hrs. No chasing.
  • Built for tier-2 India first. Gwalior today. Indore, Bhopal, Jabalpur next.
