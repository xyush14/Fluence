FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is Fluence free to join?

Yes. 100% free for both creators and brands. We earn a 5% commission only when a deal closes successfully. You pay nothing upfront, ever.

Do I need a minimum follower count?

Nope. We welcome creators from 500 followers to 500K. Brands pick who fits their budget and vibe. Small accounts often get picked for authenticity over polish.

Which cities are you in?

We're in open beta now. Join the app and your city will light up as brands come on board. Expanding by demand — the faster a city's creator pool grows, the faster we bring brands.

How do payments work?

Brand pays creator directly over UPI after content is delivered. We don't hold your money — ever. In-app escrow (optional) is coming soon via a verified Indian fintech partner.

I'm a brand — how fast can I start?

Apply via our brand onboarding flow (WhatsApp us on +91 88156 21916). Approval typically under 24 hours. First deal goes live the same day. Creators apply within hours.

What if a deal falls through?

If a brand doesn't pay, we mediate over WhatsApp and freeze them from the platform until resolved. Our reputation is our runway — we have every incentive to protect you.
